Important checks¶

Some checks need to be performed before starting the observations.

On nuraghe-mng¶

Check that :
  • all of the 32 containers are active on ACS (MNG virtual desktop) (Fig.1);
  • the active surface is green on the AS virtual desktop (Fig.2);
  • the log client jlog is open in order to track possible error messages (Fig.3). In case it is not open, type $ jlog & on a shell;
  • the interface of the Meteo client is open to check the wind velocity in real time (< 60 km/h) (Fig.4). If it is close, type $ meteoClient & on a shell.
Check the presence of the 8 panels (Fig.5):
  • operatorInput (Fig.6)
  • AntennaBoss (Fig.7)
  • GenericBackend (Fig.8)
  • Mount (Fig.9)
  • Observatory (Fig.10)
  • Receivers (Fig.11)
  • Scheduler (Fig.12)
  • MinorServo (Fig.13)

Upload your shedules (.scd, .lis, .bck and .cfg files) and check them:

From your computer:

$ scp  [schedulename.*] gavino@nuraghe-mng:/archive/schedules/[projectID]

On nuraghe-mng:

$ cd /archive/schedules/[projectID]

$ scheduleChecker [schedulename.scd]
